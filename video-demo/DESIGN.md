## Style Prompt

Create a high-energy, technical, light-canvas product film for CyVix that feels like a premium civic SaaS demo. Use Soft Pop Neo-Brutalism translated into motion graphics: rounded blocks, hard shadows, bright flat accents, bold data cards, and confident movement. The tone should be polished and practical, not cartoonish. The video must feel like a real product story, not a template.

## Colors

- Background: `#F6EFE6`
- Ink: `#2A1214`
- Electric blue: `#2F6BFF`
- Butter yellow: `#FFE38A`
- Mint: `#A7F3D0`
- Coral: `#FF8FA3`

## Typography

- Headlines and body: `Avenir Next`, `Helvetica Neue`, `Arial`, sans-serif
- Metrics and technical traces: `SF Mono`, `Menlo`, `Consolas`, monospace

## What NOT To Do

- Do not use a dark canvas or neon cyberpunk styling.
- Do not use pure black as the primary ink color.
- Do not use purple AI gradients or glassmorphism.
- Do not make the video feel like a static dashboard walkthrough.
- Do not use tiny web-safe typography or generic stock-looking UI panels.
